Survival Mode In the survival mode of Rust, players are placed in an open-world environment and must gather resources, craft items, and build structures to survive. The game is known for its harsh and unforgiving mechanics, including a persistent world where players can lose their possessions upon death. Interaction with other players is a significant aspect, and alliances or conflicts can form as players navigate the challenges of the environment.

Battle Royale Mode

Rust also introduced a separate game mode called "Rust: Battle Royale." In this mode, players face off in a last-man-standing competition within a shrinking play area. Like other battle royale games, players start with minimal equipment and must scavenge for weapons, armor, and resources as the match progresses. The goal is to eliminate other players while avoiding the ever-shrinking safe zone, pushing players closer together and intensifying encounters.
